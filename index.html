Bio

I am a 4th year honours Bachelor of Science (BSc) student in Computer Science at the University of Calgary in Alberta, Canada.

I am also a student researcher under Dr. Kangsoo Kim in the Human-X-Interaction Lab. I received the Alberta Innovates Summer Research Studentship Award to fund a project in Summer 2024, related to avatars in VR and the Proteus Effect.

Back in Summer 2023, I received the NSERC Undergraduate Student Research Award. My project explored using virtual agents in VR to improve real-world route learning. Accepted as a poster at IEEE VR 2024!

I expect to graduate in Spring 2025 and I am currently applying to PhD positions in Virtual Reality, Augmented Reality, Embodied Agents and Avatars, and Human-Computer Interaction. My CV is attached at the bottom of this webpage.

I pride myself on my insatiable curiosity, and I love to venture out of my comfort zone to tackle challenging topics such as graphics programming and tangible interfaces.

In my free time I like to hike in the rockies, go fishing, read manga, and play difficult video games (currently playing through Elden Ring).
