About Me

I am a PhD student at the College of Computing and Informatics at Drexel University, where I am advised by Dr. Tiffany D. Do in the DIVA Lab. My research is primarily in embodiment of avatars in virtual reality (VR).

I received a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science with First-Class Honours at the University of Calgary in 2025. Throughout my time there, I did research in avatars under the supervision of Dr. Kangsoo Kim as a member of the HXI Lab.

See my publications below. Work which I am particularly proud of is highlighted in purple.
