About Me

I am a PhD student at the Drexel College of Computing and Informatics, where I am advised by Dr. Tiffany Do in the DIVA Lab. My research is in embodiment of avatars in extended reality (VR/AR).

I received my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science with First-Class Honours at the University of Calgary in Spring 2025. Throughout my time there as an undergad, I was a part of the HXI Lab, where I did research in avatars under the supervision of Dr. Kangsoo Kim.

See my publications below. Work which I am particularly proud of is highlighted in purple.
